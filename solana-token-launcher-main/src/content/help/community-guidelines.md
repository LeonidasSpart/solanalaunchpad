# Community Guidelines

ZRP is built on community. These guidelines help keep our community safe and welcoming.

## Core Values

### 🤝 Respect

Treat everyone with respect. Disagreements happen, but personal attacks are not acceptable.

### 📖 Helpfulness

Share knowledge and help others. We all started somewhere.

### 💬 Transparency

Be honest and transparent. Misleading others is not allowed.

### 🔒 Safety

Keep the community safe. Report suspicious activity.

## What We Allow

### ✅ Allowed

- ✅ Constructive feedback
- ✅ Questions and discussions
- ✅ Sharing knowledge
- ✅ Reporting issues
- ✅ Feature suggestions

### ❌ Not Allowed

- ❌ Spam or promotions
- ❌ Scams or fraud
- ❌ Harassment or abuse
- ❌ Impersonation
- ❌ Illegal activities

## Code of Conduct

### Be Respectful

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| ✅ Be kind | ❌ Personal attacks |
| ✅ Listen | ❌ Interrupting |
| ✅ Help | ❌ Dismissing |

### Be Constructive

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| ✅ Give feedback | ❌ Complain without solutions |
| ✅ Ask questions | ❌ Assume ignorance |
| ✅ Share knowledge | ❌ Gatekeeping |

### Be Honest

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| ✅ Be transparent | ❌ Mislead others |
| ✅ Admit mistakes | ❌ Blame others |
| ✅ Share facts | ❌ Spread rumors |

### X (Twitter)

- **@ZRP_AI** — Official account

### Telegram

- **Main Group** — General chat
- **Announcements** — Official news

## What to Share

### ✅ Do Share

- Your created tokens
- Questions and help requests
- Feedback and suggestions
- Community events
- Success stories

### ❌ Don't Share

- Private keys or seed phrases
- Personal information
- Scams or phishing links
- Unauthorized promotions
- Inappropriate content

## Reporting Issues

### How to Report

1. **Discord** — @mod or @team
2. **X** — @ZRP_AI
3. **Email** — support@zrp.one

### What to Report

- Rule violations
- Suspicious activity
- Spam or scams
- Harassment
- Security concerns

## Consequences

| Violation | First | Second | Third |
|-----------|-------|--------|-------|
| **Minor** | Warning | Mute | Kick |
| **Moderate** | Warning | Kick | Ban |
| **Severe** | Ban | Ban | Ban |

## Being a Good Community Member

### How to Help

1. ✅ Answer questions
2. ✅ Share knowledge
3. ✅ Report issues
4. ✅ Welcome new members
5. ✅ Be positive

### How to Contribute

1. ✅ Give feedback
2. ✅ Suggest features
3. ✅ Report bugs
4. ✅ Help others
5. ✅ Share your experience
