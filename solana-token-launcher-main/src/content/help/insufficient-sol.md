# Insufficient SOL Balance

Running out of SOL is a common issue. This guide explains why you need SOL and how to get it.

## Why You Need SOL

### On Devnet

| Use | Amount Needed |
|-----|---------------|
| **Transaction fees** | ~0.000005 SOL |
| **Account rent** | ~0.002 SOL |
| **Network fees** | ~0.001 SOL |
| **Total** | **~0.01 SOL** |

### On Mainnet

| Use | Amount Needed |
|-----|---------------|
| **Transaction fees** | ~0.000005 SOL |
| **Account rent** | ~0.002 SOL |
| **Platform fee** | 0.15-0.45 SOL |
| **Network fees** | ~0.01 SOL |
| **Total** | **0.2-0.6 SOL** |

## Getting SOL

### On Devnet (Free)

#### Option 1: Solana Faucet

1. Go to [Solana Faucet](https://faucet.solana.com)
2. Enter your wallet address
3. Click **"Request Airdrop"**
4. Wait for confirmation

#### Option 2: ZRP Devnet Testing

1. Use Devnet on ZRP
2. No SOL required
3. Token creation is free

### On Mainnet (Real SOL)

#### Option 1: Buy SOL

1. Create an account on an exchange:
   - [Coinbase](https://coinbase.com)
   - [Binance](https://binance.com)
   - [Kraken](https://kraken.com)

2. Buy SOL
3. Withdraw to your wallet

#### Option 2: Swap for SOL

1. Use [Jupiter](https://jup.ag)
2. Swap other tokens for SOL
3. Receive SOL in your wallet

## Troubleshooting

### Faucet Not Working

1. Try a different faucet:
   - [Solana Faucet](https://faucet.solana.com)
   - [Helius Faucet](https://faucet.helius.dev)

2. Check your wallet address
3. Wait 24 hours (rate limited)

### SOL Not Showing

1. Refresh your wallet
2. Check the correct network
3. Wait for confirmation

### Insufficient Balance Error

1. Check your balance
2. Add more SOL
3. Try again
