# Contact Support

Need help? The ZRP support team is here to assist you.

## How to Contact Us

### Contact Form

1. Go to the [Contact page](/contact)
2. Fill in:
   - Your name
   - Email address
   - Subject
   - Message
3. Click **"Send Message"**

### Email

Send an email to:
```

support@zrp.one

```

Please include:
- A clear subject line
- Detailed description
- Relevant screenshots

### X (Twitter)

Reach out on X:
- **Handle:** [@ZRP_AI](https://x.com/ZRP_AI)
- **DM:** Open for support
- **Public:** Tag us for quick responses

### Discord

Join our Discord:
- **Link:** [discord.gg/W4qS4xkbn](https://discord.gg/W4qS4xkbn)
- **Support Channel:** Get help from the community and team
- **Direct:** DM team members

## What to Include

### For Technical Issues

| Detail | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Token address** | To identify the token |
| **Transaction ID** | To check the transaction |
| **Error message** | Exact error text |
| **Steps to reproduce** | How to replicate |
| **Screenshots** | Visual evidence |

### For Account Issues

| Detail | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Wallet address** | To identify your account |
| **Network** | Devnet or Mainnet |
| **Issue description** | What happened |

### For General Questions

| Detail | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Question** | Clear, specific question |
| **Context** | What you're trying to do |
| **Background** | What you've tried |

## What We Can Help With

### ✅ We Can Help With

- Token creation issues
- Wallet connection problems
- Transaction failures
- Technical questions
- Bug reports
- Feature requests

### ❌ We Cannot Help With

- Lost private keys
- Recovering stolen funds
- Investment advice
- Price predictions
- Trading decisions

## Reporting Issues

### Bug Reports

1. **Describe** the bug clearly
2. **Steps** to reproduce
3. **Expected** behavior
4. **Actual** behavior
5. **Screenshots** or videos

### Security Issues

If you find a security issue:
1. **Do NOT** share publicly
2. **Email:** support@zrp.one
3. **Subject:** Security Issue
