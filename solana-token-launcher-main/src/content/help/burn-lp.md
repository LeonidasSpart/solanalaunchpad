# Burning LP Tokens

Burning LP tokens is the strongest trust signal you can give to your community. It permanently locks liquidity.

## What Are LP Tokens?

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **LP Token** | Liquidity Provider token |
| **What It Does** | Represents your share of a liquidity pool |
| **How You Get It** | Received when you add liquidity |

## Why Burn LP Tokens?

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **💯 Trust Signal** | Proves you can't rug pull |
| **🔒 Permanent Lock** | Liquidity is locked forever |
| **🤝 Holder Confidence** | Attracts long-term holders |
| **✅ Commitment** | Shows you're serious |

## What Burning Does

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| You hold LP tokens | LP tokens are destroyed |
| Can withdraw liquidity | Liquidity is permanently locked |
| You're in control | No one controls it |

> ⚠️ **Important:** Burning LP tokens is permanent. You cannot recover them.

## How to Burn LP Tokens

### Step 1: Get Your LP Tokens

First, add liquidity to a DEX to receive LP tokens:

1. Go to [Raydium](https://raydium.io)
2. Add liquidity to your token/SOL pool
3. You receive LP tokens

### Step 2: Use ZRP's Burn LP Tool

1. Go to [Burn LP Tool](/burn-lp)
2. Click **"Select Wallet"**
3. Approve the connection

### Step 3: Select Your LP Token

1. Your LP tokens will appear in the list
2. Click on the one you want to burn

### Step 4: Confirm Burn

1. Review the details
2. Click **"Burn LP Tokens"**
3. Approve the transaction in your wallet

### Step 5: Verify

1. Check your wallet — LP tokens are gone
2. Check the pool — liquidity is locked

## When to Burn LP Tokens

| Timing | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Immediately** | Maximum trust from day one |
| **After Testing** | Once you're confident |
| **Before Promotion** | To show commitment |
