# Rug Pull Prevention

Rug pulls are scams where creators abandon projects and take investors' money. This guide explains how to prevent and identify them.

## What Is a Rug Pull?

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Rug Pull** | A scam where creators drain liquidity and disappear |
| **Common In** | Memecoins, new projects |
| **Result** | Holders lose their money |

## How Rug Pulls Happen

### The Process

1. **Creator creates token** with active authorities
2. **Adds liquidity** to a DEX
3. **Attracts investors** through marketing
4. **Mints more tokens** (if mint authority active)
5. **Removes liquidity** (if LP tokens not burned)
6. **Disappears** with the money

### Red Flags

| Sign | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **Mint authority not revoked** | Can create unlimited tokens |
| **LP tokens not burned** | Can remove liquidity |
| **Anonymous team** | Can disappear |
| **No community** | No real support |

### Built-in Protection

| Feature | How It Helps |
|---------|--------------|
| **Authority Revocation** | Prevents abuse |

| **Open Source** | Code is auditable |
| **Non-Custodial** | You control your keys |

### Best Practices

| Practice | Why |
|----------|-----|
| **Revoke all authorities** | Prevents abuse |
| **Burn LP tokens** | Locks liquidity |
| **Build community** | Shows commitment |
| **Be transparent** | Builds trust |

## How to Spot a Rug Pull

### Red Flags

- 🚨 Mint authority is still active
- 🚨 LP tokens are not burned
- 🚨 Team is anonymous
- 🚨 No community engagement
- 🚨 Promises of guaranteed returns
- 🚨 Pressure to buy quickly

### Green Flags

- ✅ All authorities revoked
- ✅ LP tokens burned
- ✅ Known team members
- ✅ Active community
- ✅ Clear roadmap
- ✅ Transparent communication

## Protecting Yourself

### Before Investing

1. **Check authority status** on Solscan
2. **Check LP tokens** — are they burned?
3. **Research the team** — are they known?
4. **Check community** — is it active?
5. **Read the roadmap** — is it realistic?

### After Investing

1. **Monitor authority status**
2. **Watch for suspicious activity**
3. **Stay informed** about project updates
4. **Diversify** your investments
5. **Take profits** when appropriate

## What to Do If You Spot a Rug

1. **Document everything** — Take screenshots
2. **Warn others** — Share information
3. **Report** — To relevant platforms
4. **Contact** — Seek community support
