# Token Not Showing in Wallet

Sometimes your token doesn't automatically appear in your wallet. This guide shows you how to add it.

## Why Tokens Don't Show

| Reason | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| **Not auto-detected** | Wallets don't show all tokens |
| **No liquidity** | Tokens without pools may not show |
| **Manual add needed** | Most tokens need manual addition |
| **Wrong network** | Check Devnet vs Mainnet |

## How to Add Token

### Phantom Wallet

#### Step 1: Open Phantom

1. Click the Phantom extension
2. Make sure you're on the right network

#### Step 2: Go to Tokens

1. Click **"Tokens"** tab
2. Scroll down
3. Click **"Add Token"**

#### Step 3: Enter Mint Address

1. Paste your token's mint address
2. Click **"Add"**
3. Token will appear

### Solflare Wallet

#### Step 1: Open Solflare

1. Click the Solflare extension
2. Make sure you're on the right network

#### Step 2: Go to Assets

1. Click **"Assets"** tab
2. Click **"Add Token"**

#### Step 3: Enter Mint Address

1. Paste your token's mint address
2. Click **"Add"**
3. Token will appear

### Backpack Wallet

#### Step 1: Open Backpack

1. Click the Backpack extension
2. Make sure you're on the right network

#### Step 2: Go to Tokens

1. Click **"Tokens"** tab
2. Click **"Add Token"**

#### Step 3: Enter Mint Address

1. Paste your token's mint address
2. Click **"Add"**
3. Token will appear

## Finding Your Mint Address

### On ZRP

1. Go to [Token Explorer](/tokens)
2. Click on your token
3. Copy the mint address

### On Solscan

1. Open your transaction
2. Look for the token mint
3. Copy the mint address

## Common Issues

### "Token not found"

```

Error: Token not found on this network.

```

**Solution:**
1. Check the network (Devnet/Mainnet)
2. Make sure you're on the same network
3. Try again

### "Invalid address"

```

Error: Invalid token address.

```

**Solution:**
1. Check the mint address
2. Make sure it's complete
3. Copy it again

### Logo Not Showing

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| **Logo not loading** | Wait for IPFS to propagate |
| **Wrong logo** | Check metadata |
| **No logo** | Uploaded correctly? |

## Quick Fixes

### Refresh and Retry

1. **Refresh** the page
2. **Reopen** your wallet
3. **Try again**

### Switch Networks

1. Check if you're on Devnet or Mainnet
2. Switch to the correct network
3. Try adding the token again

### Use Solscan

1. Go to [Solscan](https://solscan.io)
2. Search your token
3. See all details there

## Prevention Tips

### During Creation

1. ✅ Save your mint address
2. ✅ Verify on Solscan
3. ✅ Add to wallet immediately

### After Creation

1. ✅ Check Solscan
2. ✅ Add to wallet
3. ✅ Share with community
